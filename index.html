    • Identifying and fixing broken replication
  • DistributedCaching of alerts on database servers to reduce load on alerting APIs.
  • Usage of network maintenance APIs to do pre-emptive automatedswitchovers of database primaries during the period of low traffic.
  • MySQL Clusters DNS Migration from a deprecated platform to a new one.
  • Cost saving measures during covid to reduce MySQL Database Servers.
